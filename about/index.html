ABOUT

My job is to code and I LOVE doing it! I was born in Chico, California, raised across the world in Belgium, Malaysia, and Egypt, and now I live in the San Francisco Bay Area. Growing up, I spent a lot of time working at my dad's computer store; I did work, like building computers, but I also played, which ranged from video games to making custom electronics with computer parts. Technology is something I've always been surrounded by, turning it more into a passion or a hobby to me than a profession. Despite studying enginering initiailly, I studied public health at Johns Hopkins University because as a first-generation American, I was humbled by the disparities between my country and those of my parents. I participated , and now, I continue to help others using my skills as a programmer and participating in events like civic hack nights.
